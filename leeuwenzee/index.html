Welcome to Leeuwenzee

A relaxed home from home in the heart of Sea Point

Leeuwenzee is a comfortable, good-value guest house in one of Cape Town's friendliest neighbourhoods. We are a short stroll from the Promenade, the sea, and a buzz of restaurants, shops and cafes, with easy access to the V&A Waterfront, Table Mountain and the city beyond.

Every room is en-suite with its own kitchenette, so you can self-cater and come and go on your own schedule. Add a hearty Cape-style breakfast, cool off in the splash pool, and end the day around the braai. Whether you stay a night or a season, we make it effortless.
