Everything you need

Comforts that make it feel like home

From self-catering kitchenettes to a splash pool and braai nights, Leeuwenzee is built for relaxed, independent stays in the heart of Sea Point.

In-room kitchenettes

Cook, brew and store your own way. Every room is self-catering so you stay on your own schedule.

Outdoor splash pool

Cool off after a day on the Promenade in our sunny outdoor splash pool.

Braai facilities

Fire up a proper Cape braai in the evenings and enjoy a true South African welcome.

Co-working desks & Wi-Fi

Dedicated desks and fast, free Wi-Fi throughout, ideal for remote work and longer stays.

Cape-style breakfast

Add a hearty Cape breakfast to your stay and start the day right before exploring the city.

Flexible check-in & cleaning

Late check-in and check-out by arrangement, plus daily room cleaning so your stay stays effortless.
