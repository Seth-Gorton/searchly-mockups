Welcome to Cape Nelson

The feeling of a private home, the comfort of a fine guesthouse.

Tucked into a quiet, leafy street in upper Sea Point, Cape Nelson is a place to slow down. Every room is individually decorated, the garden is made for long breakfasts, and the Promenade, beaches and the best of Cape Town are all a short stroll or drive away.

Whether you are here for a few nights or a few weeks, you will be looked after by people who actually live and breathe this neighbourhood.

A genuine welcome, every single stay.

The neighbourhood

Sea Point, on your doorstep

One of Cape Town's most loved suburbs. Walk the Promenade at sunset, swim at the beaches, and find a hundred cafes, restaurants and shops within a few blocks.

  • A short walk to the Promenade and the Atlantic seaboard beaches.
  • Minutes from the V&A Waterfront, the city and Table Mountain.
  • Cafes, delis and restaurants all within an easy stroll.
