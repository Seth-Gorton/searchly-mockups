From our kitchen

A menu built around fire, flour and the finest produce.

Every dish at Saint starts with a craft: dough proved slowly, pasta rolled by hand, prime cuts kissed by flame. This is contemporary Italian, done with intent.

Wood-fired pizza

Blistered, leopard-spotted and gone in minutes, fired in an oven brought in from Naples for that unmistakable char.

Fresh pasta

Made daily in-house. Silky tagliatelle, pillowy gnocchi and ragus that taste of a long, slow afternoon.

Seafood & antipasti

The day's catch, carpaccio, burrata and sharing boards to open the table and set the evening in motion.

The bar

A curated cellar of wines beside crafted cocktails, rare gins and whiskeys. The place to begin, or to linger.

Sweet finales

Classic Italian desserts, reimagined. The right note to end a long evening at the table.

Private events & vouchers

Corporate dinners, weddings and birthdays styled around you, plus gift vouchers for the people who deserve them.

The Josper grill

A wood-fired steakhouse at the heart of Saint.

Our Josper charcoal grill sears at intense heat, locking in smoke and depth. Aged cuts and the freshest seafood, handled simply, so the flame does the talking.

  • Aged, prime cuts grilled over wood charcoal
  • Whole fish and seafood, flame-finished
  • Paired by the table with our cellar and bar
