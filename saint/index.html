From our kitchen

A menu built around fire, flour and the finest produce.

Every dish at Saint starts with a craft: dough proved slowly, pasta rolled by hand, prime cuts kissed by flame. This is contemporary Italian, done with intent.

Wood-fired pizza

Blistered, leopard-spotted and gone in minutes, fired in an oven imported from Naples for that unmistakable char.

Fresh pasta

Made daily in-house. Silky tagliatelle, pillowy gnocchi and ragùs that taste of a long, slow afternoon.

Josper grill

Our wood-fired charcoal grill and steakhouse. Aged cuts and seafood, seared at intense heat for smoke and depth.

Seafood & antipasti

The day's catch, carpaccio, burrata and sharing boards to open the table and set the evening in motion.

The bar

A curated cellar of wines beside crafted cocktails, rare gins and whiskeys. The perfect place to begin or to linger.

Private events & vouchers

Corporate dinners, weddings and birthdays styled around you, plus gift vouchers for the people who deserve them.
