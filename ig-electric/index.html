What we do

Electrical work for homes and businesses

From a single fault to a full rewire, IG Electric covers it all, with a Certificate of Compliance on every job that needs one.

Installations & COCs

New electrical installations and Certificates of Compliance for sales, transfers, insurance and peace of mind. Done by the book.

Rewiring, Alterations & Additions

Rewiring old or unsafe circuits, extending your wiring for renovations, and adding new points, plugs and lights safely.

Maintenance & Repairs

Geysers, pool motors, gate motors and lighting. We fix what is broken and keep everything running the way it should.

Fault Finding

Tripping circuits, dead plugs, mystery faults? We track down the real cause quickly and put it right, no guesswork.

DB & Inverter Upgrades

Distribution board upgrades plus inverter and load-shedding solutions to keep your lights, Wi-Fi and home running through outages.

Security Systems

Alarms, CCTV, electric fencing and access control, installed and wired properly so your home or premises stays protected.

Why IG Electric

A 35-year reputation, in every job we touch.

We started wiring homes and businesses across Sandton in 1986. More than three and a half decades and over 3,500 clients later, the way we work has not changed: arrive when we say we will, quote honestly, do the job properly, and leave it safe and compliant.

We are trusted across the residential, domestic, industrial and commercial sectors, and by several large corporate clients and the insurance industry. Our name is on the line every time, which is why so many clients have stayed with us for years and send us straight to friends and family.
