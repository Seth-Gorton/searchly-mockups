The opportunity

The NCCA is the recognised voice of contract cleaning in South Africa, and has been since 1989. The current website does not do that standing justice, and more importantly, it buries the one action the site exists to drive: membership.

Membership is the primary goal, yet on the current site it competes for attention with several equal-weight cards (member login, search a cleaner, search a supplier). The value proposition is vague and inward-looking ("The Voice of the Cleaning Industry") and never answers the question a prospective member actually asks: what does joining the NCCA do for my business?

On top of that, the things that would close the sale are hidden. Membership cost, categories and the application process sit inside downloadable PDFs. There is no proof near the call to action: no member count, no "since 1989", no Department of Labour recognition, no designations surfaced where a prospect decides. And the site itself is slow, image-heavy and dated, which quietly costs you applications, especially on mobile.

What we are proposing

A complete rebuild of the NCCA website around a single, focused job: get qualified cleaning companies and suppliers to apply for membership. Everything else on the site serves that goal. We keep your blue and navy brand, surface the credibility you have already earned, make the value of membership impossible to miss, and rebuild the site so it is fast, modern and easy to find in search.

What we will improve

We audited the current site against one measure: does it turn the right visitors into members? These are the specific changes that will move that number.

  • One dominant "Apply for Membership" call to action. Made the hero of the site and repeated through the page, with login and search demoted to a slim secondary row so they no longer split attention.
  • An outcome-led hero. We replace "The Voice of the Cleaning Industry" with a headline about what membership delivers, win more contracts, prove compliance, protect your business, backed by three to four concrete member benefits above the fold.
  • A trust band near the action. Established since 1989, registered professional body, recognised by the Department of Labour as an employers' association, national footprint across provinces, and the designations you award, all surfaced where a prospect decides, not buried in the nav.
  • Transparent membership categories and a simple "how to join". Clear member categories on the page (no PDF hunting) and a 3-step "how to join" section, because pricing and process transparency removes the biggest objection.
  • An inline enquiry form instead of a gated PDF. A short "request membership info / start your application" form (name, company, email, phone, province) captures the lead first, then delivers the full pack, so interested companies are never lost.
  • A proper, filterable member directory. The asset that makes membership worth paying for, rebuilt from a basic map into a filterable, profile-rich listing (by service, region and designation badge) that clients actually want to use.
  • Designations and compliance, surfaced. Your real differentiators promoted to a homepage trust pillar, with partner and accreditation logos (ISSA, Cleantex) properly labelled.
  • A faster, lighter, SEO-friendly rebuild. Compressed hero imagery, fewer fonts, no legacy or unused code, semantic structure and schema, all on a clean foundation that keeps your blue and navy brand.

How we build it

Three things set our websites apart: they are built to convert, they are built to be found, and you can see exactly what they do.

Built to convert (conversion rate optimisation)

"Conversion" means a visitor taking the action you want. For the NCCA, that is applying for or enquiring about membership. We design the whole site so a much higher share of the right visitors actually apply instead of leaving: one dominant Apply for Membership action, an outcome-led message, the right trust signals near the decision, transparent categories and process, and friction removed at every step. After launch we keep watching the numbers and tuning to lift that share further.

Built to be found: SEO fundamentals baked in

Searchly Web is the website arm of Searchly, a Google Ads and SEO focused agency. Search is what we do, so your website is built on solid SEO fundamentals from the very first line of code:

  • Clean, fast, semantically structured code search engines can read easily
  • Pages targeting how people actually search ("contract cleaning association South Africa", "is my cleaner NCCA accredited")
  • Local and national signals so the NCCA shows up across provinces
  • Organization schema and a crawlable structure that give you the best foundation to rank

This groundwork means the site is ready to perform in organic search and makes any future SEO or Google Ads work far more effective.

Built so you can see what works

We install analytics and conversion tracking focused on the thing that matters: membership enquiries. Every enquiry and application is measured, turning the website from a brochure into a tool you can improve, and powering smarter marketing decisions later.

Ownership, hosting and domain

The NCCA owns the site outright. We make the technical side simple, even though hosting is not something we provide.

Once the project is complete and paid, the website belongs entirely to the NCCA. No monthly fee to Searchly, no lock-in. It is your asset.
