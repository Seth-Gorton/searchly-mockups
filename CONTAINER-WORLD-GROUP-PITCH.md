## Conversion audits (why the redesigns) — full detail in each NOTES.md
- **Container World:** no hero value prop/H1, quote form buried on inner pages, weak imagery + grey wall, no above-the-fold service paths, scattered trust signals (ISO 9001, SAOGA, CSTA, BIC, 40 yrs, 13 branches), buried phone, dated heavy Elementor. ~13 branches, multi-division.
- **Container World Offshore:** thin ~6-page brochure for a serious DNV 2.7-1 / EN 12079 certified offshore business; no quote path, one-line product teasers (no specs/certs), zero trust signals, broken Locations page, 3 languages on one page.
- **Self Storage CW:** single thin page for a 4-city operator (Durban, Polokwane, Midrand, Nelspruit); no pricing, one generic form for 4 cities, no per-location pages (hurts local SEO + Ads), no social proof, hero typo "Tafety".
- **Self Storage CW Midrand:** single landing page; broken phone link, off-message stock hero, form buried at bottom, excl-VAT-only pricing, no map, no reviews.

## What the mockups do (all 7)
Quote-first home pages in each real brand: hero with an inline quote form, "Get a quote" primary CTA throughout + floating button, proof/trust bars, real images/logos pulled from their sites, clear pricing on storage sites (from R700/R950 per month), maps on storage/location sites, Midrand broken phone fixed. New builds (Durban/Nelspruit/Polokwane) use the Self Storage CW brand, localised per city.
