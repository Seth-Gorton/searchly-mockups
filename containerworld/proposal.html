The opportunity

Container World is the established leader: 40 years of trading, around 13 branches nationwide, multiple divisions and a brand with real equity. The website does not yet do that stature justice, and it is quietly costing you enquiries.

Buyers researching containers, conversions, reefers, offshore units or self-storage are landing on a site that does not convert. There is no hero value proposition telling them what you do and why you, the quote form is buried behind a separate page, the imagery is weak, and the trust signals you have earned over four decades are scattered rather than shown with confidence. For a national leader, the current site under-sells the business and lets warmer competitors win the contact.

What we are proposing

A complete, modern rebuild that does one job above all: it turns more visitors into quote requests. Every page is designed around your primary action, GET A QUOTE, and the whole site is rebuilt fast, credible and easy for your team to maintain, while keeping the Container World red and blue brand you have built equity in.

➜

GET A QUOTE, made effortless

We put a short quote form above the fold and a persistent GET A QUOTE button on every page and device, so a serious buyer can request pricing in seconds instead of hunting for a contact page. Removing that friction is the single biggest lever on enquiry volume, and we are leaning into it hard.

How we build it

Built to convert (conversion rate optimisation)

"Conversion" means a visitor taking the action you want, in your case, requesting a quote. We design the whole site around GET A QUOTE as the primary action, not WhatsApp: clear messaging, a service grid for fast self-selection, the trust signals you have earned, friction removed, and an obvious quote path on every page. After launch we keep watching the numbers and tuning to lift the share of visitors who convert.

Built to be found: SEO fundamentals baked in

Searchly Web is the website arm of Searchly, a Google Ads and SEO focused agency. Search is what we do, so your site is built on solid SEO fundamentals from the first line of code:

  • Clean, fast, semantically structured code search engines can read easily
  • Dedicated product and division pages targeting how buyers actually search
  • Strengthened location pages and local SEO signals across the branch network
  • Structured data and a crawlable structure that give you the best foundation to rank

This groundwork makes any future SEO or Google Ads work far more effective.

Built so you can see what works

We install analytics and conversion tracking so every quote request is measured, turning your website from a brochure into a tool you can improve, and powering smarter marketing decisions later.

Ownership, hosting and domain

You own your website outright. We make the technical side simple, even though hosting is not something we provide.

✓ The website is yours

Once the project is complete and paid, the website belongs entirely to Container World. No monthly fee to Searchly, no lock-in. It is your asset.

✓ You stay in control

Your hosting and domain stay in your name with your provider. That is deliberately better for you: full ownership and never dependent on us to stay online.

To be completely clear: Searchly Web does not provide hosting. Hosting (the service that keeps your website online) and your domain (containerworld.co.za) are held directly with a hosting provider. They are an ongoing cost paid to that provider, not to us, and are not included in the build fee.

But you will not be left to figure it out

While we do not sell hosting, we will assist you through the entire setup. We will:

  • Advise you on a suitable hosting setup, in plain language
  • Set your new website up on your hosting account
  • Point your domain correctly so your web address works as it should
  • Make sure the site goes live properly, securely and without downtime

We handle the full technical setup so the move to the new site is smooth, with your existing pages redirected correctly to protect your search rankings.
