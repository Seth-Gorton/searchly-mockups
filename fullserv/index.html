What we do

Full-service finance for growing businesses

From day-to-day bookkeeping to specialist tax, we cover the whole picture so nothing falls through the cracks.

Accounting & bookkeeping

Accurate monthly books, reconciliations and management accounts that show you exactly how the business is doing.

Tax: VAT, PAYE & income tax

Returns filed correctly and on time, plus proactive tax planning so you never pay more than you should.

Payroll, UIF & COIDA

Payslips, UIF and Workers' Compensation handled monthly, so staff are paid right and you stay compliant.

Company secretarial

CIPC filings, director changes and company records kept current, so your statutory side is always in order.

Annual financial statements

Compliant, lender-ready AFS prepared to professional standards, ready when the bank or SARS asks.

SARS expat tax

Specialist support for South Africans working abroad: tax residency, the expat exemption and getting it right with SARS.
