Massage & holistic therapy

The healing touch

Skilled, hands-on therapy to relax tight muscles, lift your mood and rebalance body and mind.

Swedish Massage

A variety of techniques designed to relax muscles, using kneading, stroking, friction and tapping in the direction of blood returning to the heart.

Back, Neck & Shoulder

Targeted relief for the areas that carry the most tension, relaxing muscles and easing everyday stress and strain.

Hot Stone & Gua Sha

Smooth heated basalt stones as an extension of the therapist's hands. The heat relaxes tight muscles, aids absorption of essential oils and rebalances the body.

Aromatherapy Massage

The art of helping the body towards balance using essential oils extracted from plants, with healing properties for skin, mood and soul.

Detoxifying Massage & Wrap

Thalasso therapy using red and brown seaweed and marine clay from the West Coast of France, rich in minerals to stimulate the metabolism and lymph drainage.

Lymphatic Drainage

Gentle, effective therapy for cellulite, swollen feet and ankles and water retention. A lightness of body is experienced almost immediately.
