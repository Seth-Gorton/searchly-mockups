Body contouring & slimming

Target fat, tighten skin, no downtime

High-intensity ultrasound energy is focused on the targeted fat. The energy passes through the skin to destroy fat tissues without harming the surface of the skin. Over the next few days and weeks the body naturally removes the destroyed fat, leaving you with a thinner, smoother and more contoured shape.

  • Eliminate fat on targeted areas
  • Tighten and smooth loose skin on soft or ageing areas
  • Visibly target stubborn cellulite
  • Stimulate lymphatic drainage

Incredible results for skin laxity in the abdominal area caused by pregnancy and weight loss, and exceptional for tightening sagging knees and arms. The radio frequency results are immediate and continue in the months following treatment.

Gentle on the surface

Deep heating, with your skin kept safe

The radio frequency heats the deep layers while it cools the epidermis, which keeps the surface of the skin safe during the procedure. As a result your skin needs no recovery time and you can quickly resume your daily routine.

That deep heating tightens the collagen in your dermis and subcutaneous tissue, while also stimulating the formation of new collagen. You will see tighter, smoother skin and improved tone and texture. These results start immediately and continue in the months following treatment.
