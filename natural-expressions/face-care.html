Our face care menu

Treatments for every skin concern

From signature Guinot Hydradermie to advanced peels and non-surgical blemish removal, here is what we offer and what each one does for you.

Guinot Facials

The signature Hydradermie uses double-ionised galvanic current to drive active ingredients deep into the skin, for instant youthfulness and long-term hydration. The Guinot range also includes Aromatic, Liftosome, Age Summum and Beaute Neuve for lifting, firming and radiance.

Book a Guinot facial →

Dermalogica Facials

It starts with Face Mapping, a deep analysis of your skin's health, so every step is customised to you. Choose the Classic Facial, Age Smart for visible firming and smoothing, Medibac Clearing for acne, or the Ultra Calming Facial for sensitised, reactive skin.

Book a Dermalogica facial →

RegimA Peels

The most advanced, medically accepted South African skincare programme, made for doctors and professional therapists alone. Safely, gently and naturally, RegimA restores the skin's youthful appearance, improving colour, tone and texture and smoothing fine lines, whatever your age.

Book a RegimA peel →

Microdermabrasion

A diamond-tip microdermabrasion that targets the signs of ageing, sunspots, hyperpigmentation, enlarged pores, blackheads, acne, scarring and dullness. Used on its own or as a precursor to another treatment, it brings about intense, visible results.

Book microdermabrasion →

Lamprobe Blemish Removal

Our speciality treatment. Lamprobe is a non-invasive, precise procedure using radio and high-frequency technology to safely remove benign lesions, fibromas, skin tags, keratosis, broken capillaries and more. Non-surgical, quick, safe and effective.

Ask about Lamprobe →

Eye & Speciality Treatments

Targeted care for the most delicate skin, including Guinot's patented Eye Logic to lift and tone the eye area, reduce dark circles and puffiness, plus electrolysis for permanent facial hair removal. Ask us what is right for your concern.
