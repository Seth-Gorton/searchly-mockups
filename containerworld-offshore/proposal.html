The opportunity

You are a substantial, 40-year offshore and marine container business. Your website does not yet say so. That gap is costing you quotes.

Container World Offshore supplies DNV 2.7-1 and EN 12079 certified offshore equipment to oil, gas and marine operators across Africa and beyond, backed by an established group operating since 1983, multiple branches, and an extensive certified product range. That is exactly the credibility an international industrial buyer is looking for.

But the current site is a thin brochure: products are reduced to one-line teasers with a "Read More", there is no real quote path (only a generic "Contact Us"), and there are no trust signals at all, no certifications shown as badges, no "since 1983", no countries served, no proof. A high-value buyer comparing certified suppliers lands, cannot self-qualify or request a quote on specific units, and leaves. This is a clear, high-leverage B2B conversion gap.

What we are proposing

A complete rebuild that does two jobs at once: it presents Container World Offshore as the credible, certified, internationally-relevant supplier it actually is, and it makes requesting a quote effortless. Every page is built to lead a serious buyer toward a quote on specific units, with a persistent GET A QUOTE action as the primary route throughout the site.

✉

A real quote path, not a generic contact form

We build a purpose-built quote system: a prominent, persistent GET A QUOTE button in the header and on every product, leading to a quote form with the fields a buyer actually needs (product type, quantity, certification, destination port or country, hire or buy, timeline). "Contact" becomes the secondary action. This is the single biggest lever for more qualified leads, and we are leaning into it hard.

How we build it

Three things set our websites apart: they convert, they put a real quote path first, and they are built to be found.

Built to convert (conversion rate optimisation)

"Conversion" means a visitor taking the action you want, in your case, requesting a quote on specific units. We design the site so a much higher share of buyers actually request a quote instead of leaving: a buyer-led message, certifications and trust shown where decisions are made, friction removed, and an obvious next step on every page. After launch we keep watching the numbers and tuning to lift that share further.

GET A QUOTE as the primary action

For an industrial buyer comparing certified suppliers, the desired action is a quote on specific products and quantities, not a chat. We make GET A QUOTE impossible to miss: a persistent header button and a per-product quote button that pre-fills the form, so a serious buyer can request a quote in seconds. We keep phone and WhatsApp as supporting routes, but the quote system is the hero, not WhatsApp.

Built to be found: SEO fundamentals baked in

Searchly Web is the website arm of Searchly, a Google Ads and SEO focused agency. Search is what we do, so your website is built on solid SEO fundamentals from the very first line of code:

  • Clean, fast, semantically structured code search engines can read easily
  • Substantive per-product pages targeting how buyers actually search ("DNV offshore containers", "certified cargo baskets")
  • Proper multilingual handling (hreflang, localised paths) for Francophone and Lusophone markets
  • Product and organisation schema plus a crawlable structure that give you the best foundation to rank

This groundwork means your site is ready to perform in organic search and makes any future SEO or Google Ads work, through our sister agency Searchly, far more effective.

Built so you can see what works

We install analytics and conversion tracking so every quote request is measured, turning your website from a brochure into a tool you can improve, and powering smarter marketing decisions later.

Ownership, hosting and domain

You own your website outright. We make the technical side simple, even though hosting is not something we provide.

✓ The website is yours

Once the project is complete and paid, the website belongs entirely to Container World Offshore. No monthly fee to Searchly, no lock-in. It is your asset.

✓ You stay in control

Your hosting and domain stay in your name with your provider. That is deliberately better for you: full ownership and never dependent on us to stay online.

To be completely clear: Searchly Web does not provide hosting. Hosting (the service that keeps your website online) and your domain (containerworldoffshore.com) are held directly with a hosting provider. They are an ongoing cost paid to that provider, not to us, and are not included in the build fee.

But you will not be left to figure it out

While we do not sell hosting, we will assist you through the entire process. We will:

  • Advise you on a suitable hosting setup, in plain language
  • Set your new website up on your hosting account
  • Point your domain correctly so your web address works as it should
  • Make sure the site goes live properly, securely and without downtime

The good news on cost: hosting and a domain are not expensive, and we will confirm the exact, modest figure with you so there are no surprises.
