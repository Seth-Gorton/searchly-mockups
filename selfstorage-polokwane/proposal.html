How we build it

Three things set our websites apart: they convert, they put getting a quote first, and they are built to be found in local search.

Built to convert (conversion rate optimisation)

"Conversion" means a visitor taking the action you want, in your case, getting a quote for a unit. We design the site so a much higher share of visitors actually enquire instead of leaving: transparent pricing that answers the first objection, the right trust signals, friction removed, and an obvious next step on every section. After launch we keep watching the numbers and tuning to lift that share further.

Get a quote as the primary action (not WhatsApp)

For a storage branch, a qualified quote request is the lead that turns into a rental. We make GET A QUOTE the hero of the site: a quote form in the hero, clear get-a-quote buttons on every section, and click-to-call alongside. The whole journey funnels toward that single action, so a serious enquirer gives you exactly what you need to follow up and close. Less friction means more quote requests, and more quote requests mean more units rented.

Built to be found: local Polokwane SEO baked in

Searchly Web is the website arm of Searchly, a Google Ads and SEO focused agency. Search is what we do, so your Polokwane site is built on solid SEO fundamentals from the very first line of code:

  • Clean, fast, semantically structured code that search engines read easily
  • A page built around how people actually search ("self storage Polokwane", "storage units Polokwane")
  • Local SEO signals, address, map and LocalBusiness structured data, so you show up for storage searches in Polokwane
  • A crawlable structure that gives the branch the best foundation to rank locally

This groundwork means the Polokwane site is ready to perform in organic search and makes any future SEO or Google Ads work far more effective.

Ownership, hosting and domain

You own your website outright. We make the technical side simple, even though hosting is not something we provide.

✓ The website is yours

Once the project is complete and paid, the website belongs entirely to Self Storage CW. No monthly fee to Searchly, no lock-in. It is your asset.

✓ You stay in control

Your hosting and domain stay in your name with your provider. That is deliberately better for you: full ownership and never dependent on us to stay online.

To be completely clear: Searchly Web does not provide hosting. Hosting (the service that keeps your website online) and your domain (your web address) are held directly with a hosting provider. They are an ongoing cost paid to that provider, not to us, and are not included in the R5 000 build fee.

But you will not be left to figure it out

While we do not sell hosting, we will assist you through the entire process. We will:

  • Advise you on a suitable hosting setup, in plain language
  • Set your new Polokwane website up on your hosting account
  • Point your domain correctly so your web address works as it should
  • Make sure the site goes live properly, securely and without downtime

The good news on cost: hosting and a domain are not expensive, typically around R1 000 a year, and we will confirm the exact, modest figure with you so there are no surprises.
