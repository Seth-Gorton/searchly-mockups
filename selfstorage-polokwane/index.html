What fits in a unit

How much can one unit hold?

Each unit is a full 20ft container, roughly the floor space of a single garage. Here is a rough guide so you can pick the right space before you book.

A flat or small move

The contents of a one or two-bedroom flat: bed, lounge suite, table, fridge, washer and around 20 to 30 boxes, with room to spare.

A house in transit

Packed carefully, a unit comfortably holds the furniture and boxes of a three-bedroom home while you move, renovate or relocate.

Business stock and tools

Retail stock, documents, equipment, seasonal inventory or tools of the trade, kept dry, secure and close to hand for your business.
