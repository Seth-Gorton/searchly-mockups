The opportunity

Self Storage CW is a real multi-location operator, the self-storage arm of Container World, renting secure converted-container units across four cities: Durban, Polokwane, Midrand and Nelspruit. The website does not yet reflect that strength.

Today the entire public presence is a single, thin page. It does not publish what a unit costs, it runs one generic enquiry form for all four cities, it has no dedicated page per location, and it shows no reviews or trust proof at all. For a trust-led purchase like storage, where the first question is always "what does it cost and is my stuff safe?", that quietly sends ready-to-rent visitors to competitors who answer those questions on the page.

What we are proposing

A complete rebuild as a fast, modern, multi-location lead-generation site: one indexable landing page per branch, transparent pricing, a quote form that routes to the right city, sticky call and WhatsApp, and a clear "how it works to reserve" path. Every page is designed to lead a serious enquirer to one action: GET A QUOTE and reserve a unit, kept in your familiar blue and red brand.

☉

The single biggest lever: publish your pricing

Storage shoppers compare on price first. Right now your prices (from R700 per month for top-level units, from R950 for ground-floor) are hidden away on a separate Midrand microsite. We bring them onto the main site and every location page, sitting right next to a "Get a quote" button, so visitors qualify themselves instead of bouncing to a rival who shows the number.

You have already seen the direction. The live concept mockup (seth-gorton.github.io/searchly-mockups/selfstorage-cw) shows the look, feel and structure. The current photos in it are your existing assets used as placeholders. As soon as we have fresh branch photography, we apply it so each location looks unmistakably like its real site. It is a concept to react to, not the finished website.

What we will improve

We audited your current site against one question: does it help a visitor get a quote and reserve a unit? These are the fixes that will move the needle most.

  • Publish clear pricing. A simple, honest pricing block (from R700 per month for top-level units, from R950 for ground-floor, with what is included) on the homepage and every location page, next to a reserve action.
  • Location-routed quote form and per-location landing pages. A page for each branch (Durban, Polokwane, Midrand, Nelspruit) with its own address, map, photos, price, hours and contacts, and a form that routes the lead to the correct branch inbox. Far better local SEO and a clean message-match for Google Ads.
  • A prominent GET A QUOTE / reserve action. An obvious primary call to action on every page and a sticky call and WhatsApp bar, so a hot lead reaches the right branch in seconds rather than hunting in the footer.
  • How it works and a size guide. A clear 3 to 4 step "how to reserve" path and a "what fits in a unit" guide, so first-time renters can self-qualify and you receive better, more confident enquiries.
  • Trust and security proof. We surface the things that win a trust purchase: 24-hour monitored security (CCTV, electric fencing, access control), the steel anti-vandal, watertight, vermin-proof build, the Container World heritage, and Google reviews and testimonials once gathered.
  • A fast, modern rebuild in your brand. A lightweight, responsive site that keeps your blue and red identity, replaces the dated 2019 layout, and loads quickly for both visitors and search.
One quick win we will fix on day one. The live site's page title currently reads "A Self Storage Experience of Trust & Tafety", a visible typo ("Tafety") that hurts both credibility and search. We correct it as part of the rebuild, along with rewriting the hero to lead with the concrete promise: secure 20ft container units across four cities, from R700 per month, 24/7 monitored.

How we build it

Three things set our websites apart: they convert, they make getting a quote effortless, and they are built to be found.

Built to convert (conversion rate optimisation)

"Conversion" means a visitor taking the action you want, in your case, getting a quote to reserve a unit. We design the site so a much higher share of visitors actually enquire instead of leaving: published pricing, the right trust signals, friction removed, and an obvious next step on every page. After launch we keep watching the numbers, per branch, and tuning to lift that share further.

Get a quote as the primary action

The whole site points to one thing: a quick, easy quote. We make the "Get a quote" button impossible to miss and back it with a sticky call and WhatsApp bar, with the right branch contact for the visitor's city. WhatsApp and a quick call are how South African storage enquirers prefer to reach a business, so we make both effortless, while the quote form stays the clear primary path that captures the lead and routes it correctly.

Built to be found: SEO fundamentals baked in

Searchly Web is the website arm of Searchly, a Google Ads and SEO focused agency. Search is what we do, so your website is built on solid SEO fundamentals from the very first line of code:

  • A dedicated, indexable landing page per city, the single biggest local-SEO win for a four-branch operator
  • Pages targeting how people actually search ("self storage Durban", "container storage Midrand", and each city)
  • LocalBusiness structured data and a clean, crawlable structure so each branch can rank in its area
  • Fast, lightweight code that supports both organic ranking and Google Ads Quality Score

This groundwork means the site is ready to perform in organic search and makes any future SEO or Google Ads work far more effective.

Tracking that earns its place (important for your Ads spend)

Much of your traffic is likely Google Ads driven, so clean measurement is not optional. We install GA4 and conversion tracking on the quote form so every enquiry is counted, and tagged to the right branch and campaign. That turns your website into a tool you can improve, and means each city's Ads spend is guided by real data rather than guesswork.

Ownership, hosting and domain

You own your website outright. We make the technical side simple, even though hosting is not something we provide.

✓ The website is yours

Once the project is complete and paid, the website belongs entirely to Self Storage CW. No monthly fee to Searchly, no lock-in. It is your asset.

✓ You stay in control

Your hosting and domain stay in your name with your provider. That is deliberately better for you: full ownership and never dependent on us to stay online.

To be completely clear: Searchly Web does not provide hosting. Hosting (the service that keeps your website online) and your domain (your web address, selfstoragecw.co.za) are held directly with a hosting provider. They are an ongoing cost paid to that provider, not to us, and are not included in the build fee.

But you will not be left to figure it out

While we do not sell hosting, we will assist you through the entire process. We will:

  • Advise you on a suitable hosting setup, in plain language
  • Set your new website up on your hosting account
  • Point your domain correctly so your web address works as it should
  • Make sure the site goes live properly, securely and without downtime

The good news on cost: hosting and a domain are not expensive, typically around R1 000 a year, and we will confirm the exact, modest figure with you so there are no surprises.
