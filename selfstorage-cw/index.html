Size guide

What fits in a unit?

Each unit is a standard 20ft container: 6.05m long, 2.44m wide and 2.59m high, roughly 14.8 square metres of secure, dry floor space. That is plenty for a home move or a growing business. As a rough guide, one unit comfortably holds:

  • The contents of a 2 to 3 bedroom home
  • A full lounge, beds and appliances
  • Business stock, files and equipment
  • Garden tools, bikes and outdoor gear
  • Furniture during a move or renovation
  • Seasonal and overflow storage
