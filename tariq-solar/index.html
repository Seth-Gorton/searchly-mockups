What we do

Complete solar and backup power, done properly.

From a single inverter and battery for load-shedding to a full grid-tied solar system that slashes your bill, we design every job around your home, your usage and your budget.

Solar panel design & installation

Properly sized, neatly mounted panel arrays engineered for your roof and your real-world consumption, for maximum yield.

Inverter systems

Sunsynk and leading-brand inverters, sized and configured correctly so your system runs efficiently and reliably for years.

Battery backup solutions

Quiet, clean lithium battery backup that keeps your lights, Wi-Fi and essentials running straight through every outage.

Grid-tied solar systems

Generate your own power by day and stay connected to Eskom, the smartest way to cut your monthly electricity bill.

Backup power for load-shedding

Stop the disruption. We install dependable backup so load-shedding and unplanned outages stop costing you time and money.

Maintenance & support

We do not disappear after install. Friendly local support, monitoring and servicing to keep your system performing.
