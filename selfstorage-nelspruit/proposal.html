How we build it

Three things set our websites apart: they convert, they drive quote requests rather than aimless clicks, and they are built to be found.

Built to convert (conversion rate optimisation)

"Conversion" means a visitor taking the action you want, in your case, requesting a quote for a unit. We design the site so a much higher share of visitors actually enquire instead of leaving: a clear price up front, the right trust signals, a simple "how it works", friction removed, and an obvious GET A QUOTE on every section. After launch we keep watching the numbers and tuning to lift that share further.

GET A QUOTE as the primary action (not WhatsApp)

For storage, the lead you want is a qualified quote request you can follow up and convert into a signed unit. So we make the quote the hero of the site, a short form in the hero and repeated down the page, with click-to-call alongside it. We lead with the quote rather than WhatsApp so every enquiry lands as a real, trackable lead with the details you need to respond fast and reserve the unit.

Built to be found: SEO fundamentals baked in

Searchly Web is the website arm of Searchly, a Google Ads and SEO focused agency. Search is what our sister agency does, so your website is built on solid SEO fundamentals from the very first line of code:

  • Clean, fast, semantically structured code search engines can read easily
  • Content targeting how local renters actually search ("self storage Nelspruit", "storage units Mbombela")
  • Local SEO signals and LocalBusiness data so you show up for storage searches in Nelspruit
  • Structured data and a crawlable page that give you the best foundation to rank

This groundwork means your site is ready to perform in organic search and makes any future SEO or Google Ads work far more effective, and far cheaper per lead.

Built so you can see what works

We install analytics and conversion tracking so every quote request is measured, turning your website from a brochure into a tool you can improve, and powering smarter marketing decisions later.

Ownership, hosting and domain

You own your website outright. We make the technical side simple, even though hosting is not something we provide.

✓ The website is yours

Once the project is complete and paid, the website belongs entirely to Self Storage CW. No monthly fee to Searchly, no lock-in. It is your asset.

Your hosting and domain stay in your name with your provider. That is deliberately better for you: full ownership and never dependent on us to stay online.

To be completely clear: Searchly Web does not provide hosting. Hosting (the service that keeps your website online) and your domain (your web address) are held directly with a hosting provider. They are an ongoing cost paid to that provider, not to us, and are not included in the R5 000 build fee.

But you will not be left to figure it out

While we do not sell hosting, we will assist you through the entire process. We will:

  • Advise you on a suitable hosting setup, in plain language

  • Point your domain correctly so your web address works as it should

The good news on cost: hosting and a domain are not expensive, typically around R1 000 a year, and we will confirm the exact, modest figure with you so there are no surprises.
