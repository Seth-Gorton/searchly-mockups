Our story

Internationally recognised, proudly Johannesburg.

The Friendly Plant has designed and built distinctive gardens since 2006. From our base in Muldersdrift we serve Greater Johannesburg's premier suburbs and estates, including Sandton, Bryanston, Steyn City, Waterfall and Blair Atholl, and we have completed projects across 18 countries.
