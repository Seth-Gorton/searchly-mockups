What we do

A complete garden studio, from concept to canopy.

One team, one vision. We design the space, source the plants and hardscape, and build it beautifully, so you deal with experts who own the whole result.

Turnkey landscaping

Full garden creation and planting design, managed start to finish. We handle everything from soil to specimen trees so the finished garden is exactly as imagined.

Landscape design

Water-wise, indigenous, formal, tropical, contemporary or zen. We design to your site, your light and your life, with mood, structure and year-round interest.

Water features & fountains

Reflective pools, rills and fountains that bring sound, movement and calm to a space, designed and engineered to last and to suit the garden's character.

Outdoor kitchens & patios

Entertainment spaces built for South African living: paved patios, built-in kitchens and seamless indoor-to-outdoor transitions for effortless gatherings.

Fire pits & bomas

Gathering places designed around the fire. Bomas and fire pits crafted as the warm heart of the garden, beautiful by day and magical after dark.

Estate-grade delivery

We work fluently within the rules and standards of premium estates, coordinating access, approvals and trades so the build runs smoothly from day one.
