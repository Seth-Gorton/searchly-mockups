The opportunity

Your Midrand page is a single-location landing page in the Container World group, and it is almost certainly receiving Google Ads traffic. The problem is not that people are not arriving. It is that the page is quietly letting them leave.

We audited the live site in detail. It carries genuinely good raw material: a secure, floodlit, CCTV-monitored facility, real container photos, and honest pricing from R700 a month. But the page works against itself in a handful of ways that each cost you enquiries:

  • The phone link is broken, so on mobile, tapping the number does nothing. A primary way to enquire is simply dead.
  • The hero is an off-message stock photo (a man behind a desk fan) with a vague pun, instead of your real, secure containers, your location and your price.
  • The only enquiry form is buried at the very bottom of a long page, with a stray validation label leaking into it and no reassurance about what happens next.
  • Pricing is shown excl VAT only, with no inc-VAT figure and no size guide, which reads as a hidden cost and leaves the buyer guessing what fits.
  • There is no map and no social proof: no exact Midrand address to confirm where they would store, and zero reviews or trust signals on what is fundamentally a trust purchase.

In short, you are likely paying for clicks that the page then fails to convert. That is the single best kind of problem to fix, because the demand already exists. We just need to capture more of it.

How we build it

Three things set our pages apart: they convert, they make enquiring effortless, and they are built to be found.

Built to convert (conversion rate optimisation)

"Conversion" means a visitor taking the action you want, in your case, requesting a quote or reserving a unit. We design the page so a much higher share of the visitors you are already paying for actually enquire instead of leaving: a strong hero, clear pricing, the right trust signals, friction removed, and an obvious next step on every screen. Get a quote and reserve a unit are the primary action throughout, with calling as a clear secondary path, not a WhatsApp-led page.

✉

The quote form is the hero, with calling close behind

For a single-location storage page, the fastest win is a short quote form above the fold backed by a working tap-to-call. We make requesting a quote the easiest thing on the page, and we fix the broken phone link so a buyer who would rather call can reach you in one tap. Less friction means more enquiries, and more enquiries mean more units rented.

Built to be found: SEO fundamentals baked in

Searchly Web is the website arm of Searchly, a Google Ads and SEO focused agency. Search is what our sister agency does, so your page is built on solid SEO fundamentals from the very first line of code:

  • Clean, fast, semantically structured code search engines can read easily
  • Copy targeting how customers actually search ("self storage Midrand", "storage units Midrand", and nearby areas)
  • Local SEO signals so you show up for storage searches in your area
  • Structured data and a crawlable structure that give you the best foundation to rank

This groundwork means the page is ready to perform in organic search and makes your Google Ads spend work harder, because the page it lands on actually converts.

Built so you can see what works

We install analytics (GA4) and Google Ads conversion tracking so every quote enquiry and call is measured. That turns the page from a brochure into a tool you can improve, and lets your Ads budget be spent on what actually brings in renters.
