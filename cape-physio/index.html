A physiotherapist can help if you

Recognise any of these?

Easing or correcting everyday musculoskeletal pain. If one of these sounds like you, a single WhatsApp message starts the path to relief.

Lower back pain

Often caused by weak core muscles and poor sitting or standing posture, eased with hands-on therapy and targeted stretching.

Shoulder stiffness

From poor posture and repetitive arm movement such as long stretches of driving or desk work.

Wrist & hand pain

Usually repetitive strain from keyboards and non-standard hand positions held for long periods.

Migraines & headaches

Frequently driven by tension in the head, neck and eyes, which physiotherapy can help release.

Stiff neck

Pain or stiffness at the base of the head and neck that can radiate into the shoulders and back.

Upper & mid back pain

Weak back muscles from inactivity and long hours sitting, set off by a single wrong movement.

Sports & orthopaedic injuries

Sprains, strains, fractures and arthritis, assessed and rehabilitated to restore mobility.

Neurological rehabilitation

Stroke recovery and conditions affecting balance, coordination and movement.

Meet your therapist

Personal, expert care from someone who treats how you move, work and live

Cape Physio is led by Fatima Hartley, who has been treating patients in Sea Point since 2010. With a rare dual qualification in physiotherapy and occupational therapy, both from UCT, Fatima treats not just the symptom but the whole pattern behind it, so the results last. She has years of experience in stroke rehabilitation, post-surgery recovery and general rehabilitative care, and has lectured biomechanics at the University of Cape Town.
