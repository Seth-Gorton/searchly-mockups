Meet your therapist

Personal, expert care from someone who knows your story

Cape Physio is led by Fatima Hartley, who has been treating patients in Sea Point since 2010. With a dual qualification in physiotherapy and occupational therapy, Fatima treats not just the symptom but the way you move, work and live, so the results last.

  • BSc Physiotherapy and BSc Occupational Therapy, both from the University of Cape Town.
  • Recognised with a WhatClinic Service Award for outstanding patient care.
  • Over 14 years of one-to-one care for the Sea Point community.
