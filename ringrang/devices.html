Good to know

Device questions, answered

Do the phones come set up?

Yes. Every handset, headset and conference phone is pre-configured for your Ring Rang account before it reaches you. Plug it in, connect it to your network, and it is ready to call.

Can I buy or rent the devices?

Both. You can buy a device outright, or rent it as part of your monthly plan so the cost is spread out. Tell us which you prefer and we will quote accordingly.

What about warranty and support?

Devices carry a manufacturer warranty, and our team supports the setup so your phones work the way they should. If something is not right, we help you sort it out.

Can I use my own VoIP phone?

Yes. If you already have a compatible VoIP phone, you can connect it to Ring Rang. Send us the make and model and we will confirm the settings to get it talking to your account.

Ready to add phones to your plan?

Pick the handsets and headsets you need, or start with the app and add hardware later. We will set it all up for you.
