Good to know

Questions, answered

Can I keep my existing number?

Yes. Porting your existing number to Ring Rang is free, and we handle the process for you. You can also choose a new 087 business number.

How long does setup take?

Minutes. Ring Rang is self-service: choose a package, set up your team and start calling from the app. Add desk phones any time, pre-configured and ready to plug in.

Do I need to buy phones?

No. You can use the Ring Rang app on your phone and laptop with no hardware at all. Handsets and headsets are optional upgrades if you prefer them.

Is there a contract?

No lock-in contracts. Simple monthly pricing, and you can change your plan or add extras as your team grows.

Will it work during load-shedding?

Ring Rang is cloud based, so as long as a team member has a working connection (mobile data or backup power), calls keep flowing.
